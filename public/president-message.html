PRESIDENT MESSAGE

Respectable members of High Court Bar Association Rawalpindi elected me as a President for the year 2023-2024 with a remarkable difference of votes and after taking over the charge of office of the president, I have made every effort for the welfare of the Bar and also for betterment of the legal fraternity.

I express my gratitude towards the office bearers in general and Secretary in particular of High Court Bar Association Rawalpindi without whose co-operation it would have been very different for me to achieve any objective and serve the Bar with my fully ability to great extent.
